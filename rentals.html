Rentals - Overview

Location, Location! Ideally situated

Stay at The Kingdom Resort & experience this playground of the North West Province including amazing attractions like Sun City and Pilanesberg National Park less than 5km away.

Chalets

The use of natural materials and earthy tones ensures that the chalets blend in with the serene setting. Choose from 2 and 3 bedroom, self-catering, luxury chalets all fully furnished and boasting an amazing kitchen and entertainment area. Perfect for families.

‹ ›

Villas

The luxurious villas feature skillfully designed interiors furnished with solid wood tables, granite top kitchen areas, comfortable and stylish designed bedrooms, air-conditioning and DStv. Choose from 2, 3 and 4 suite, self-catering, up-market villas with top class finishes and own private splash pool and fire pit.

‹ ›

Hotel Suites

Perfect for corporates, tours or a romantic getaway. Single and Double Suite options with DStv, air-conditioning, tea and coffee making facilities with private balcony. Each suite has smartcard key access.
