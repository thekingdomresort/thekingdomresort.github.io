Overview - Facilities

The Kingdom Resort makes full use of the sunny Pilanesberg climate by providing guests with a selection of complimentary on-site facilities to keep everyone entertained.

Resort Facilities

  • Welcome Centre
    An on-site sales office with qualified sales staff that can assist you with information on investing at The Kingdom Resort.
  • Splash Kingdom Waterpark
    Cool down and have fun at our awesome waterpark.
  • Kingdom Adventures experience

    Quad bikes on resort
    Quad biking is another enjoyable way to explore the resort grounds. Please enquire at Reception.
  • Body Bliss Day Spa
    Relax, be pampered and indulge in some beauty treatments to melt your stress away.
  • Acacia Terrace Café
    Open 7 days a week between 7am and 9pm, the café serves a variety of light meals, breakfast as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.
  • Jungle Gym
    Made from plastic and with educational features, it's safe for children to enjoy for hours on end.
  • Trampoline
    Shaded and surrounded with a safety net, the trampoline is suitable for kids of all ages.
  • Adventure Golf
    With water and rock features, it's a scenic course suitable for all ages.
  • Volley Ball
    Play in the designated sand area and challenge your friends and family to a tournament.
  • Bicycle Hiring
    Visit our Reception Desk and rent one of our bicycles and explore the resort grounds.
  • Swimming Pool
    Custom designed to mimic a rock pool, it has a slide for kids, an island for sun bathing and a waterfall feature.
  • Outdoor Cinema
    A large outdoor movie wall has been created for guests to enjoy a selection of movies whilst picnicking under the stars as well as viewing popular sports events.
  • Pizzeria
    The on-site café, Acacia Terrace, now has a Pizzeria, perfect for grabbing something delicious after a day full of activities!
  • Jumping Pillow
    An internationally popular attraction that has recently been introduced to our country and showcased at selected resorts. Guests young and old love this new take on the traditional trampoline.
  • Jungle Bar And Lounge
    An African lounge experience, serving guests with a variety of drinks while they relax and unwind.
  • Rock Climbing Wall
    An 8 metre, multi-challenge, climbing wall has been built behind the brand new outdoor cinema.
  • Outdoor Table Tennis
    Table tennis is situated outdoors by the pool area.
  • Free shuttle service to Sun City!
    A complimentary shuttle service that runs periodically to and from Sun City. Booking is essential.
  • Archery
    Try your hand at the ancient sport of Archery and master this skill with a unique bow. Experience the ancient art of archery with the help of modern day equipment. Competent instructors will help you master the technique of shooting a re-curve bow.
  • Boma
    Relax, enjoy a braai and meet and mingle with other guests at the Boma.
  • Kids Play Area
    Situated around the Acacia Terrace Café area, little ones will have fun in the kids' pool, jungle gym, playground area and trampoline.
  • Mountain Bike Trail
    Hire one of our mountain bikes and explore the resort grounds. We have marked trails to make the most of your excursion.
  • Restaurant
    Savour sumptuous breakfast buffets, delicious lunches and dinners at the on-site Acacia Terrace Restaurant.
  • WiFi
    With our improved WiFi signal, sharing those holiday selfies has never been easier!
