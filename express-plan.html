Shared ownership of a chalet for 12 Years

Our Express Plan has been designed for those of you who are attracted to the concept of timeshare ownership, yet prefer a more limited period of ownership. As an owner of a week/s at a fully furnished self-catering chalet, you will have exclusive usage time of your chalet over the same season, every year for a total of 12 years, without having to worry about availability. Additional flexibility, as detailed below, is provided through the Beekman Managed Portfolio which you will automatically have access to.

Perhaps, you may prefer to have timeshare ownership for a much longer period.

Exchange Your Usage Time For Holidays Worldwide or for Rental Income

Should you not be able to use your week yourself during any year, you have the option of exchanging it for a more convenient time at The Kingdom Resort, or for accommodation at another resort both within South Africa and worldwide. Alternatively, you have the option of exchanging your unwanted usage time in order to participate in The Beekman Managed Portfolio Rental Pool.
