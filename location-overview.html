Location - Overview

Set in the crater of a long extinct volcano, the Pilansberg region of South Africa's North West Province is a feast for the eyes. It is no wonder then that this setting was chosen for the creation of the Pilanesberg National Park, the 4th largest in Southern Africa and home to the "Big 5". Here you will find syenite koppies, thickly forested ravines, bushveld, rolling grassland & wooded areas, all of which is home to The Kingdom Resort.

From Johannesburg

  • Travel north along William Nicol to the Fourways intersection at Witkoppen Road.
  • Turn left into Witkoppen Road passing Fourways Mall on your right hand side.
  • At first set of traffic lights, turn right into Cedar Avenue. Proceed for 8.5km to T-junction and Lion Bridge supermarket will be on your right.
  • Turn left and then take the first right-hand turn, proceed for 2km to T-junction passing over the R28 freeway.
  • Head for the N4 then take the R556.
  • Turn right at the off-ramp, Continue on the R556 for approximately 70km.
  • Entrance to The Kingdom will be on your left, 5km from Sun City.

From Pretoria

  • Head for the N4 then take the R556.
  • Turn right at the off-ramp, Continue on the R556 for approximately 70km.
  • Entrance to The Kingdom will be on your left, 5km from Sun City.
